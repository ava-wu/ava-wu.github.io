About

Hi, there, I'm Ava. I am a researcher and recent UC Berkeley graduate with diverse interests in science, language, law, and performing arts.

Currently, I am the Assistant Lab Manager / International Privacy Literacy Project Coordinator for the Interdisciplinary Research Group on Privacy, at UC Berkeley. Outside of the lab, I am an event supervisor and test writer with Science Olympiad.

I am also a language nerd. English is my first language. I have studied French for nearly a decade, and I obtained my degree in it too. I learned Modern Greek for two years. Mandarin and Taiwanese are my heritage languages. Thanks to my environment and the similarities among Romance languages, I can passably understand Spanish.

In my spare time, you can find me engaging in the performing arts. Or Netflix and video games. I'm human too.
